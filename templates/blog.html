How It Works

Discover how our Medicine Recommendation System predicts diseases and provides personalized medical advice based on your symptoms.

Step 1: Symptom Input

Users enter their symptoms into the web application using either text input or voice recognition. The system supports a wide range of symptoms to cover various diseases.

Step 2: Machine Learning Prediction

Our system uses a pre-trained Support Vector Classifier (SVC) model that analyzes your symptoms and predicts the most likely disease in real-time.

Step 3: Disease Information

The system provides detailed information about the predicted disease, including its description, symptoms, and severity.

Step 4: Personalized Recommendations

Our platform offers tailored recommendations such as medications, precautions, diet plans, and workout tips specific to the predicted disease to guide users towards better health management.

Step 5: User-Friendly Experience

Designed with an intuitive interface and voice input support, our system ensures a seamless experience for all users.
